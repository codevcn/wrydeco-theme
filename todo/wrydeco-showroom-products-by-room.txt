Sources:
- classify-collections-by-room(1).txt: maps each room to category/style groups.

Rules used:
- A room contains the union of products from all mapped collections assigned to that room.
- Products are deduplicated by Shopify product ID inside each room.
- Explore All Pieces and New Arrivals are excluded because they are not room category collections.
- Empty collections contribute zero products.
- Category/style entries without a corresponding collection in the treemap are listed separately.
- Product names below are exact Shopify handles from the treemap.
