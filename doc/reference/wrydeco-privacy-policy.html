Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate our website, remember preferences, understand website usage, improve performance, and, where enabled and permitted, support marketing and advertising. Marketing Communications

If you subscribe to our marketing communications, we may send emails about WRYDECO products, collections, custom work, stories, offers, and updates. You may unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link in an email or by contacting support@wrydeco.com. We may still send non-promotional messages about your orders, transactions, account, support requests, or policy updates.

Data Retention & Security

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide our services, maintain business and transaction records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information; withdraw consent; opt out of certain marketing, targeted advertising, or data sharing; and request information about how we collect, use, and disclose personal information.

To exercise a privacy right, email support@wrydeco.com with the subject line Privacy Request. We may need to verify your identity before responding. We will not discriminate against you for exercising rights available under applicable law.

International Transfers, Children & Third-Party Links

We may process, store, or transfer personal information in countries other than where you live. Where required by law, we take reasonable steps to ensure that information transferred internationally receives appropriate protection.

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can review the matter.
