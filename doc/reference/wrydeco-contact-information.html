05

Custom Furniture and Personalization Inquiries

WRYDECO offers customization options for selected handcrafted wood products. To request a custom product or modification, please provide as much information as possible:

  • The type of product you are interested in
  • Your preferred dimensions
  • Your preferred wood species, color, or finish
  • Reference images, sketches, or design inspiration
  • Your intended use and installation location
  • Your delivery city, state, ZIP code, and country
  • Your preferred completion date
  • Your estimated budget, if available
  • Any special installation or access requirements

After reviewing your request, our team may contact you for additional information before confirming product availability, estimated pricing, production time, and delivery options.

Submitting a customization inquiry does not create a confirmed order or guarantee that WRYDECO will be able to fulfill the requested design.
