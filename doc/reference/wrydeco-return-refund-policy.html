The character of natural wood

Natural wood has inherent variation in grain, tone, knots, texture, movement, and organic form. Minor differences between product photography and the finished handcrafted piece are part of its character and are not considered defects.

The product is non-returnable, but if the product arrives damaged or defective, you may request a refund or replacement.

04

How to Report an Issue

Please contact support@wrydeco.com within 7 calendar days of delivery and include the following information.

1

Your full name, order number, and a clear description of the issue.

2

Clear photos of the item, all packaging, the shipping label, and any visible damage.

3

Video evidence where the issue is best demonstrated by video.

Please preserve the item and packaging. Do not discard, return, repair, alter, install, or modify the item before WRYDECO has reviewed your claim and provided written instructions. Keeping all original packaging may be necessary for a carrier claim.

05

Approved Claims & Return Instructions

A return is not required unless WRYDECO specifically instructs you to return an item. For an approved claim involving verified damage, defect, or a fulfillment error, we will provide the applicable return or collection instructions and cover approved return shipping costs.

06

Refund Timing

If a refund is approved, it will be issued to the original payment method. Please allow up to 10 business days after approval for processing. Your bank, card issuer, or payment provider may require additional time to post the refund to your account.

07

Order Cancellations

Once production has started, custom and made-to-order orders cannot be cancelled or changed. The product is non-returnable, but if the product arrives damaged or defective, you may request a refund or replacement.
